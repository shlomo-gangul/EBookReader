Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

BookReader is a free, open-source book reading application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

BookReader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Reading progress - Your current page and bookmarks for each book
  • Reader preferences - Font size, reading mode, and other display settings
  • Offline books - Books you download for offline reading are stored in your browser's IndexedDB
  • Uploaded files - PDF, EPUB, and MOBI files you upload are processed locally

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser or app settings.

Third-Party Services

BookReader connects to the following services to provide book content:

  • Project Gutenberg (gutenberg.org) - Free public domain books
  • Open Library (openlibrary.org) - Book metadata and content
  • Internet Archive (archive.org) - Book content and formats
  • Gutendex (gutendex.com) - Project Gutenberg search API

These services have their own privacy policies. BookReader only requests book content and metadata from these services - no personal information is shared.

Analytics

BookReader does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

BookReader does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our project repository.
