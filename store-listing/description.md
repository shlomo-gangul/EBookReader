# BookReader - Play Store Description

## Title
BookReader - Free Books & Reader

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Free book reader with millions of books. Read offline with a beautiful experience.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

BookReader gives you access to millions of free books from Project Gutenberg, Open Library, and the Internet Archive - all in a beautiful, customizable reading experience.

**KEY FEATURES**

- Search and browse millions of free public domain books
- Beautiful page-flip reading experience with realistic animations
- Multiple reading modes: Day, Night, and Sepia
- Customizable fonts, font sizes, and line spacing
- Download books for offline reading - no internet required
- Bookmark your favorite pages
- Upload your own PDF, EPUB, MOBI, and AZW3 files
- Smooth swipe gestures for natural page turning
- Scroll mode for continuous reading
- Progress tracking across your library

**READING EXPERIENCE**

Choose between flip mode with realistic page-turn animations or scroll mode for continuous reading. Customize your experience with four font families, adjustable font sizes, and three reading modes designed to reduce eye strain.

**YOUR LIBRARY**

Browse popular books by genre, search by title or author, and build your personal library. BookReader remembers your reading progress so you can pick up right where you left off.

**OFFLINE READING**

Download any book for offline reading. Perfect for commutes, flights, or anywhere without reliable internet access.

**FORMATS SUPPORTED**

- PDF files
- EPUB files
- MOBI / AZW3 files (Kindle format)
- Books from Project Gutenberg, Open Library, and Internet Archive

**FREE AND OPEN**

BookReader is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no tracking. All books are sourced from public domain collections.
